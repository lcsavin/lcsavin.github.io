About Me

Hello and welcome to my site! My name is Lilly Carter Savin. I'm from West Palm Beach, Florida and am a senior at Virginia Polytechnic Institute & State University, majoring in multimedia journalism and minoring in technical and professional writing. Throughout my academic career, I've studied the habits of online users and took a specific interest in misinformation and disinformation — including its causes and potential solutions — after seeing this list of fake news websites, some of which I had been using for my own school work! Thanks to Virginia Tech, I've also had the privilege to conduct A/B tests involving Twitter engagement for three major news distributers and have completed various content audits on multiple existing sites. My passion, however, is using the power of communication and a journalistic approach to solve problems and deliver truth to my community. My biggest inspiration is my sister Charlotte (Charlie) who is my constant reminder to take risks and believe that any story, no matter how small, deserves to be told!
